About BTES

BTES- Launched in 2008, bebo Technical Education Services Pvt. Ltd. is bebo Technologies’ first ever finishing school in the tricity. Also known as bebo’s Educational Foundation.

This non profitable organization was started with the solo objective to serve the community by training the candidates in the northern region and provide them with placement opportunities. BTES creates a talent pool for the parent company (bebo Technologies Pvt. Ltd.) and is successfully able to meet around 90% of bebo’s requirement for freshers.

We are the pioneers for software testing training in Chandigarh and cover a varied list of programs such as SQE, SDET, API Testing, Java, Android Development, Python, Ruby.

In order to meet this objective, BTES works in coordination with the industry to bridge the gap between the industry and the academia, thereby keeping the pace with the latest emerging trends in the industry. We have experienced and qualified trainers to provide quality training to the students. BTES caters to the suitably designed trainings for both the working professionals as well as industrial trainings for college students.

We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified and process oriented company. Our major emphasis is on providing the quality trainings through our strong processes and high standards.
